# PrepareAI Launch Campaign — "Panic to Prepared"

**The thesis:** every viral prep video on the internet is either panic-content ("my exam is tomorrow") or flex-content ("how I passed"). PrepareAI is the bridge between them — so every piece of content shows the same 3-beat arc: **PANIC → the AI builds the plan → CONFIDENCE**. The product's screen IS the content; no production budget needed.

**The three filmable magic moments** (screen-record on prepareai.co):
1. **The Morph** — landing page asks "What are you preparing for?", you tap, the whole page rebuilds itself for your event.
2. **The Bloom** — you type your event + date, and a 20-node mindmap of your entire preparation blooms around it.
3. **The Mercy Rule** — you drag the understanding slider to 😵, type "too much jargon", and the AI rewrites the lesson with a completely different explanation in 30 seconds.

Nobody else has moment #3. It anchors the campaign.
